Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about shopping with iRun.

What is Click & Collect?

Click & Collect lets you order online and pick up your items at any iRun store. Simply choose Click & Collect at checkout, select your preferred store, and we'll have your order ready â€” usually within 2 hours. You'll receive an email as soon as it's ready for collection.

Can I choose which store to collect from?

Yes. During checkout, you can pick any iRun store from the list. You can also check product availability at specific stores before placing your order.

How do I check if an item is in stock at a store?

On any product page, tap Check store availability. Select your size, then search for a store by address or use your current location. You'll see real-time stock levels for each nearby store.

Do I have to select a store every time I visit?

No. Once you choose a store, we save it to your profile. Your preferred store will be used automatically for stock checks and Click & Collect orders until you decide to change it.

Is there a fee for Click & Collect?

Click & Collect is completely free. In fact, you'll save the standard €2.99 shipping fee by picking up in store.

When can I pick up my order?

As soon as your order is ready, we'll send you a confirmation email. Most orders are ready within 2 hours during store opening hours. You'll have 7 business days to collect your order.

What happens if I don't collect my order in time?

If your order isn't picked up within 7 business days, it will be automatically cancelled. Any payment will be refunded to your original payment method within 14 days.

What do I need to bring for collection?

Bring a valid photo ID and your order number (you'll find it in your confirmation email). If someone else is collecting on your behalf, they'll need a written authorization from you, their own ID, and the order number.

Can someone else pick up my order?

Yes. They'll need a signed authorization letter from you, a valid photo ID, and your order number. You'll find an authorization template attached to your pickup confirmation email.

Can I pay when I pick up my order?

Currently, all orders must be paid online during checkout. Payment at the store is not available for Click & Collect orders.

What is your return policy?

We offer free returns within 30 days of purchase. Items must be unused and in their original packaging. You can return items to any iRun store or by shipping them back using the prepaid return label included in your order.

How much does shipping cost?

Standard shipping costs €2.99. Orders over €80 qualify for free shipping. Click & Collect is always free regardless of order value.
